About the Clinic

Where Science Meets Skin Care

V Viva Skin and Hair Clinic is a dedicated dermatology centre in the heart of Jubilee Hills. We combine clinical diagnostics with advanced treatment infrastructure to deliver results that are medically sound and genuinely visible.

Not a beauty salon, not a hospital — we occupy a thoughtful middle ground: the attentive, personalized care of a specialist clinic backed by the technology of a modern facility.

Every consultation begins with an honest assessment — Dr. Potluri evaluates your skin or scalp condition, medical history, and goals before recommending any procedure.

Appointment-based access ensures focused attention — no rushed visits, no overcrowded waiting rooms. We respect your time and your skin.

Located at Aditya Ram Square, Road No. 36, Jubilee Hills — a convenient, accessible address at the centre of Hyderabad's premium residential hub.

Clinical Approach

Dr. Neelima Potluri practices evidence-based dermatology at V Viva Skin and Hair Clinic, bringing a diagnostic-first approach to every patient interaction. She does not prescribe or recommend any treatment without a thorough assessment of skin type, lifestyle factors, and patient expectations.

Specializations

Dr. Potluri has particular experience in managing hair fall disorders — including androgenetic alopecia and diffuse thinning — using a combination of diagnostic trichoscopy, targeted therapies, and PRP protocols. In aesthetic dermatology, she specializes in acne scar correction, chemical resurfacing, and laser-based skin rejuvenation with a focus on achieving natural, appropriate outcomes.

Patient Care Philosophy

Dr. Potluri believes in frank, honest consultation — explaining what a treatment can and cannot achieve, managing expectations clearly, and never recommending procedures for their own sake. Her 4.6 Google rating across 66 reviews reflects consistent, trust-based patient relationships.

Treatment Facility

Advanced Treatment Infrastructure

Modern equipment paired with clinical expertise — so that every procedure is performed with precision, safety, and efficacy.

01 💡

Laser Platform

Medical-grade laser systems for skin resurfacing, pigmentation correction, scar reduction, and targeted hair removal. Calibrated by treatment type for precise, safe delivery.

02 🔬

Diagnostic Instruments

Trichoscopy for scalp and hair follicle analysis. Skin analysis tools that guide evidence-based treatment decisions — not guesswork.

03 💉

PRP Processing Unit

Dedicated centrifuge and PRP preparation station for hair restoration and skin rejuvenation procedures, following strict sterility protocols.

04 🛋

Private Consultation Room

Dedicated consultation space designed for focused, unhurried patient interaction — full clinical history, condition assessment, and treatment planning.

05 🧴

Chemical Peel Suite

Clinical-grade peeling agents, from AHA/BHA superficial peels to TCA-based medium depth treatments, administered with dermatologist oversight.

06 🛡

Safety & Sterilization

All procedures follow strict clinical hygiene standards. Single-use instruments where required. Allergy and patch testing available prior to chemical treatments.

All treatments at V Viva are performed under direct dermatologist supervision — no technician-led procedures.
